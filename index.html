Admission Overview

Start Your Journey With Us

EduNexus School welcomes applications from students across all backgrounds who demonstrate a commitment to academic excellence and personal growth. Our holistic admission process is designed to identify motivated learners who will thrive in our collaborative environment.

We evaluate each application on its individual merit, considering academic performance, character references, and an in-person interaction session. All admissions are subject to availability of seats in the respective grade and compliance with eligibility criteria set by the board.

Our digital-first process ensures a smooth, paperless experience — from filling out your application form to receiving your confirmation letter. Parents can track their application status in real time through the EduNexus parent portal at any stage.

Eligibility Criteria

Who Can Apply?

Our admission criteria are designed to ensure the right fit for both the student and our academic community.

🎂

Age Requirements

  • Grade 1: Child must be 5+ years as of June 1
  • Grade 6: Age between 10–12 years required
  • Grade 9: Minimum age of 14 years
  • Grade 11: Minimum age of 16 years required
  • Age proof via birth certificate is mandatory
🎓

Academic Qualifications

  • Minimum 60% aggregate in previous class exams
  • Transfer Certificate from prior school required
  • No academic backlog or repeated grades
  • English proficiency test for Grade 6 and above
  • Science and Math scores evaluated for Grade 9+
📋

General Requirements

  • Valid residence proof within the school district
  • Parent/guardian must attend orientation session
  • Medical fitness certificate from a registered doctor
  • Completed online application before the deadline
  • Non-refundable application fee of ₹500

Frequently Asked Questions

For Grades 1–5, there is no formal entrance exam. For Grades 6 and above, a basic aptitude and subject interaction session is conducted to understand the child's learning level — it's evaluative but not eliminatory.
The application processing fee is ₹500 and is non-refundable. This fee covers administrative processing and applies once per applicant per academic year. It does not count toward the admission or tuition fee.
We follow government guidelines for reserved category allocations (SC, ST, OBC, EWS). Applicants must submit valid category certificates at the time of application. Seats under each category are filled on merit within the respective category.
Applications submitted after March 31, 2025 will not be considered for this academic year. You may register your email for early notifications for 2025–26. Late admissions are only considered if seats remain after the main round.
We strongly encourage digital submission for faster processing. However, if you face technical difficulties, you may visit the school's admission office between 9 AM – 4 PM on weekdays. Original documents are verified during the in-person session.
