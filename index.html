✦ FAQs
Frequently Asked Questions
Is there a limit on the number of attempts for the entrance test? ▼
The entrance test can be attempted only once per academic year per class. If a student is not selected, they may reapply in the following year's admission cycle.
Are mid-year admissions accepted? ▼
Mid-year admissions are considered on a case-by-case basis and only if seats are vacant. The standard document requirements still apply, along with a no-objection letter from the previous school.
What is the medium of instruction? ▼
The primary medium of instruction is English. Hindi is taught as a compulsory second language from Class I onwards. Sanskrit / Gujarati is offered as a third language option.
Is there a hostel facility available? ▼
Currently we do not offer hostel accommodation. The school is a day school. We do have a transport facility covering major routes in Surat city.
How is the entrance test conducted? ▼
The written entrance test is conducted at the school campus and covers English, Mathematics, and General Science. Duration is 90 minutes. For Class XI–XII, a subject-specific interview follows for Science and Commerce streams.
Can the registration fee be refunded? ▼
The ₹500 registration fee is strictly non-refundable regardless of selection or withdrawal of application. However, tuition fees paid after receiving the offer letter are refundable within 30 days.
What is the student-to-teacher ratio? ▼
We maintain a 25:1 student-to-teacher ratio in primary classes and 30:1 in secondary classes, ensuring personalised attention for every student.
Are there any reservations / quotas? ▼
We comply with the RTE Act: 25% seats are reserved for EWS/DG category. Additionally, 5% reservation is provided for differently-abled students and 3% for management quota as per state norms.
Still have questions? We're here to help.
Our admissions team is available Monday–Saturday, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM. Drop by or reach out anytime.
